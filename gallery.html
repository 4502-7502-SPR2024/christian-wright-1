Emily Wright, Owner of Three Creeks Farm

Emily, despite initial plans of city life, now manages Three Creeks Farm, specializing in pesticide-free produce. Located near Columbia for efficient distribution, Emily and Paul acquired the land in 2020. Emily's extensive farming experience worldwide contributes to the farm's success. A fortuitous offer led them to acquire land for their organic vision.

Christian Wright, 2024
