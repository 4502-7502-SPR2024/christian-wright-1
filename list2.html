7 Best Resteraunts and Bars in Columbia, Missouri

Columbia Missouri is a great college town with plenty of great food for all to enjoy. I have been living here for four years, and I have developed a few places that I love to go to all the time. I will be putting my list in order from 1-7. This list is opinion based and not based off any data.

Shakespeare's Pizza

A great Family orientated restaurant that is known around the world. Great pizza and great drinks offered

225 S. Ninth Street Columbia, MO 65201
(573) 449-2454

Addison's

A great place to go to a higher end dinner for a date or with family. Great food options and great drinks. I really love their pork nachos!

709 Cherry Street, Columbia, MO 65201
(573) 256-1995

El Oso Mexican Grill

This is my favourite Mexican Resteraunt in Columbia. It has great prices, great tacos, and great margaritas.

522 E Broadway, Columbia, MO 65201
(573) 256-2855

Campus Bar and Grill

A great place for college kids to enjoy some drinks and good food. Offers a great bar vibe, but also great food. They have great deals on almost every day.

304 South 9th Street, Suite 100, Columbia, MO 65201
(573) 256-2855

Room 38 Restaurant and Cocktail Lounge

This is a great restaurant to go on a date, and have some good food and drinks. It is a little more high end, so it is more expensive, but definitely worth it.

38 N 8th St, Columbia, MO 65201
(573) 449-3838

Gumby's Pizza

A less family orientated pizza place, with great drinks and pool! Always has great deals, and even has special deals for St. Louis Blues games.

1201 E Broadway, Columbia, MO 65201
(573) 874-8629

Billiards on Broadway

This is a great place to play pool. They have fantastic food and drinks, and is always a fun place to be.

514 E Broadway, Columbia, MO 65201
(573) 449-0116
